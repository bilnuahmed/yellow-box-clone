Is PNP and Express Entry the same?

The difference between Express Entry and Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) immigration programs is that if applicants obtain permanent residence through the Express Entry Program, they can move anywhere in Canada except to their first landing in Quebec, because Quebec manages its own immigration program i.e. Quebec Selected Skilled Worker Program (QSSW), whereas if you are granted permanent residence through the Provincial Nominee Program (PNP), you will be required to live in the same state for a period of time that varies from state to state.
